About Amazon

Amazon, founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994, is a global e-commerce and technology giant headquartered in Seattle, Washington.
Initially an online bookstore, it has since expanded into a wide array of products and services, including cloud computing (Amazon Web Services), streaming media (Amazon Prime Video), artificial intelligence, and more.
Amazon is known for its customer-centric approach, rapid delivery services, and innovations like Alexa and Kindle.
It has also ventured into physical retail with Whole Foods Market and Amazon Go stores. As one of the world's largest companies by market capitalization, Amazon plays a significant role in the global economy.
